# Welcome to Multi-Lang Coach! 🚀

This is a "Big Data-driven Voice Conversation Coach" designed specifically for IT professionals and business people.
Here, you can practice **English** or **Japanese** workplace conversations based on your job and daily tasks, effectively accumulating your personal vocabulary and practical patterns!

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Settings & API Activation (Very Important)

By default, the system uses the "Mock Conversation Mode" to demonstrate the basic flow. To unlock powerful AI features, please follow these steps to configure:

1. Click on **[Settings & API]** at the bottom of the left menu.
2. Select the **AI Provider** you want to use and enter the corresponding API key and Model Name (Model ID):

> **💎 Option A: Google Gemini (Default Recommendation)**
> 1. Go to [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey) to log in.
> 2. Click **"Create API key"** > **"Create API key in new project"**.
> 3. Copy the string starting with **AIza** and paste it back into this system.
> *(Note: Gemini API currently provides generous free quotas for general users, no credit card required!)*

> **⚡ Option B: Groq (Ultra-fast Response Experience)**
> 1. Go to [GroqConsole](https://console.groq.com/keys) to log in.
> 2. Click **"Create API Key"**.
> 3. Copy the generated key `gsk_...` and paste it back into the system.
> 4. **Model Name (Model ID)**: Please ensure you fill in the correct model name. The default recommendation is `llama-3.1-8b-instant` or `llama-3.3-70b-versatile`, which brings a blazingly fast experience!

3. On the same page, you can also set your **Main Category**, **Topic & Role**, **Language Level**, and your **Target Learning Language**.
4. After configuring, click "Save & Return".

---

## 🗺️ Step 2: Start Your Learning Journey

The design of this system revolves around an efficient learning cycle: **"Task Selection ➔ Conversation Practice ➔ Knowledge Analysis ➔ Review & Export"**.

### 1. Daily Tasks (Dashboard)
- Enter **[Daily Tasks]**, and the system will list 6 "Scenario Tasks" of varying difficulties based on your role (from simple work reports to difficult technical negotiations).
- **Start Practice**: Click "Start Practice" on any card to enter the conversation dedicated to that scenario.
- **✨ Free Chat Mode**: Don't want to be limited by topics? Click **"✨ Free Chat Mode"** at the top of the task list to chat with the AI about recent events or discuss the latest trends freely, training your most authentic on-the-spot reactions!

### 2. Chat Practice Mode
- **Voice Input**: Click the "Microphone 🎙️" button to speak your reply directly using your voice.
- **Auto-Read**: After the AI replies, the system will read it aloud automatically (can be turned off in Settings). If you want to listen again, click the "Read Aloud" button below each message.
- **💡 Recommended Patterns**: Above the chat window, you can expand a panel to view common patterns used by native speakers suitable for the scenario. Try using them in your replies! *(Note: This panel is automatically hidden in Free Chat Mode)*
- **✨ AI Suggestions**: Even if you brainstorm in your native language or input incomplete draft words, just click the "Magic Wand 🪄" next to the chat box, and the AI will translate or polish it into the most native expression based on the context.
- **🔍 Analyze Sentence & Vocabulary Extraction**: Encounter an AI reply you don't understand? Click "Analyze Sentence" below the message, and the AI will break down the grammar in detail and automatically extract key vocabulary for you, saving it to your notebook!
- **📥 Export & AI Evaluation**: After having a conversation with the AI, click the "Export" button at the top of the screen. You can choose to export the chat log directly as a PDF, or choose "Export & Analyze" to have the AI score your overall speaking performance, grammar, and tone, and provide summary advice!

### 3. Vocabulary and Pattern Notebook
- Vocabulary corrected by the AI during conversations, or the essence extracted via "Analyze Sentence," will be automatically saved in your **[Vocabulary Notebook]** and **[Pattern Notebook]** on the left.
- You can click the pronunciation button to review at any time, or click "Export" to pack them into a PDF file and print them out.

> [!WARNING]
> **⚠️ Data Storage Notice**: Your notebook data is stored in your "browser's local storage". If you clear the browser's cookies or cache data, your notes will be lost! It is recommended that you **regularly use the "Export" function** to back up the vocabulary and patterns you've painstakingly collected as PDF files.

### 4. Common Patterns Overview
- Enter the **[Common Patterns]** page, and the system will compile all selected patterns for the current scenario for you.
- **Version Switch**: You can freely switch between the "Textbook Version (Beginner)" and the "Advanced Business (Native Thinking)" at the top to experience different levels of expression.
- **Pattern Practice**: Click "Pattern Substitution Practice" on any pattern card, and the system will activate coach mode, give you a native scenario, and train you to flexibly apply words into the pattern!

## 💡 Advanced Tips
- **Select Text to Analyze**: During the conversation, you can directly "highlight and select" a specific word or phrase in an AI message. The system will pop up a "Mark Analyzed" button, allowing you to study that specific term in depth.
- **Grammar Strictness**: If you just want to practice speaking courage, you can choose "Communication First (only major errors)" in the settings; if you are about to face an important interview or presentation, switch to "Super Strict (all minor flaws)" mode, and the AI will catch all your subtle unnatural expressions.
